Xiaodong Cheng - About

Bio

Experience

  • Research Associate, 2020 - present
    Control Group, Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge, UK
    Work with Prof. Ioannis Lestas on the project Analysis and Control of Large Scale Networks

  • Postdoc researcher, 2019 - 2020
    Control systems group, Department of Electrical Engineering, Eindhoven University of Technology, the Netherlands
    Work with Prof. Paul M.J. Van den Hof on the project Data-Driven Modeling in Dynamic Networks.

Education

  • PhD in Systems and Control (distinction cum laude), 2014 - 2018
    Jan C. Willems Center for Systems and Control, ENTEG, Faculty of Science and Engineering, University of Groningen, The Netherlands.
    Thesis: Model reduction of network systems with structure preservation, Supervisor: Prof. Jacquelien M. A. Scherpen.

  • B.S. and M.S. in Control Engineering, 2007 - 2014
    School of Electronics and Information, Northwestern Polytechnical University, China

  • I am the recipient of the Paper Prize Award from the IFAC Journal Automatica for the best paper published in the triennium 2017-2019 and the 2020 IEEE Transactions on Control Systems Technology Outstanding Paper Award.

  • Broadly, my research lies in the direction of modeling and control in networked systems and other complex systems, which have a wide range of applications including power grids, networked robots, and biochemical reactions. I am particularly interested in the topics: reduced-order modeling (model reduction), data-driven modeling (system identification), and distributed/decentralized control.
