Teaching and Supervision of Students

Teaching Assistant and Co-Lecture

  • From 1-11-2019 to 02-02-2020: I co-lectured the master course Model Reduction (5LMA0) together with Prof. Siep Weiland in Eindhoven University of Technology. I taught two modules on reduced-order modeling of complex network systems.

  • From 2015 to 2018: I was a teaching assistant for the bachelor tutorial course Mechatronics at the University of Groningen. This course is taught by Prof. Jacquelien M.A. Scherpen.

Co-Advising

  • From Jan. 2019, I co-advised Shengling Shi, a PhD student at Eindhoven University of Technology, together with Prof. Paul M.J. Van den Hof.
    His PhD topic is “Topological aspects in identification of dynamic networks”.

  • From Jan. 2019, I co-advised Mayukh Samanta, a master student at Eindhoven University of Technology, together with Prof. Siep Weiland. His intership project is on “Clustering-Based Model Reduction for Heat diffusion systems”.

  • In 2018, I co-advised Ilse Attema, a bachelor student at the University of Groningen, together with Prof. Jacquelien M.A. Scherpen.
    Her bachelor thesis is: “Fast simulation of integrated circuits using balanced truncation and low-rank approximation”.

  • In 2017, I co-advised Emma Gort, a master student at the University of Groningen, together with Prof. Jacquelien M.A. Scherpen and Prof. Bayu Jawardhana.
    Her master thesis is: “Reduced modeling of power grid dynamics using moment matching”.

  • In 2017, I co-advised Benjamin Wentink, a bachelor student at the University of Groningen together with Prof. Jacquelien M.A. Scherpen.
    His bachelor thesis: “Balanced truncation of complex electrical networksusing Penzl algorithm”.
