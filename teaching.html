Teaching and Supervision of Students

Teaching Assistant and Co-Lecture

  • From Nov. 2019 to Feb. 2020: I co-lectured the master course Model Reduction (5LMA0) together with Prof. Siep Weiland in Eindhoven University of Technology. I taught two modules on reduced-order modeling of complex network systems.

  • From 2015 to 2018: I was a teaching assistant for the bachelor tutorial course Mechatronics at the University of Groningen. This course is taught by Prof. Jacquelien M.A. Scherpen.
